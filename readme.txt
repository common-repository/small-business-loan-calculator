== Description ==

Small business loan calculator calculates the estimated loan amount that you can qualify for by entering the total annual revenue that your business generates.
This tool allows business owners that are looking to borrow money, to get an idea on how much they would qualify to borrow.  It is a great tool for a business loans company to publish on their website. If you have any questions in regards to this calculator or if you have any special requests then please contact Golden Financial Services by emailing paul@goldenfs.org

== Installation ==

Upload the small business loan calculator plugin to your blog, Activate it, then write the short code [business_calculator] to display the calculator on any page of the website.
